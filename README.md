## About

I work at the intersection of product engineering and education. My work includes production apps, private client systems, public learning material, and internal tooling built over several years of hands-on development.

- CEO of [Capdesis](https://capdesis.com/), focused on software, education, and automation.
- Instructor for programming, Git/GitHub, Vim, Photoshop, and language fundamentals.
- Builder of products such as Formulae, Ingenieria Tracker, Capmenu, Cap Living, Lo Mas Fresh, CapTienda, and OmniMon.
- Personal site: [jorgesalgadomiranda.com](https://jorgesalgadomiranda.com/).
